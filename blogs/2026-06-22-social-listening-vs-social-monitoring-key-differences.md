Distinguishes social monitoring, the real-time tracking of brand mentions, tags, comments, and direct messages across social channels, from social listening, which analyzes sentiment, conversations, and trends across platforms, forums, blogs, and review sites over time. Explains when brands should use each to protect reputation and shape product strategy, campaign direction, and competitive positioning.
